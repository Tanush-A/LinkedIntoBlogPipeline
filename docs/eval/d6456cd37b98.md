**What Risks Do Data Gaps Pose to AI Revenue Forecasting?**

Disjointed datasets are the root of misleading AI outputs. When AI operates on incomplete data, it extrapolates conclusions that feel definitive but are fundamentally flawed. These missteps cascade into critical business areas, skewing revenue projections and hindering effective strategy. For instance, absent data on pipeline shifts or buyer intent can distort your quarterly projections, misleading the entire team.

**How Does Data Normalization Improve AI Precision?**

Consistency across datasets is paramount for accurate AI outputs. Data normalization aligns disparate information sources, like CRMs and emails, into a coherent format, ensuring AI leverages complete and reliable data. This process curtails AI's reliance on conjecture and enhances decision-making accuracy. With normalized data, every sales forecast becomes a dependable tool for strategic planning rather than a speculative gamble.
